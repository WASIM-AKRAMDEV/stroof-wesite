ROOFING MATERIALS

We use quality materials like asphalt, metal, and TPO for diverse roofing needs.

ASPHALT/ COMPOSITE

An asphalt shingle roof is the most common type of roof seen on homes today. It is made up primarily of asphalt shingles and other asphalt roofing components.

CLAY TILE

Clay tile roofing is a premium option that offers durability, longevity, and beauty. They are also resistant to fire, wind, and weather, and add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any home or building.

SLATE TILE

An asphalt shingle roof is the most common type of roof seen on homes today. It is made up primarily of asphalt shingles and other asphalt roofing components.

SLATE TILE

Highly durable and long-lasting material, and they are resistant to fire, wind, and extreme weather. Slate tile roofing is made from slate rock, and is also highly sought after for its unique and natural appearance.

TPO/SINGLE PLY

TPO single-ply roofing is a cost-effective, energy-efficient and durable roofing solution. It’s a thermoplastic roofing membrane that is highly resistant to UV rays, chemicals and punctures, making it a suitable option for flat and low-sloped roofs.

SPANISH TILE

A perfect combination of beauty and functionality, Spanish tile roofing is a premium option that adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to any home or building. It’s made from clay and offers durability and longevity.

WOOD SHINGLES/ SHAKES

Wood shingle/shake roofs are natural, attractive, but require regular maintenance and are less fire-resistant than other materials.

CORRUGATED METAL

STANDING SEAM METAL Standing seam metal roofing is made from metal and features raised seams that add a unique, attractive design to any home or building. It’s also weather-resistant and energy-efficient, making it a smart investment for any property.
